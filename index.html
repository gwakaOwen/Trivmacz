About Trivmacz

More than just an internet cafe - we're a community hub in Tzaneen

Meet Alice

Founded by Alice with a vision to bring reliable, high-speed internet access to Tzaneen, Trivmacz Internet Cafe has become a cornerstone of our community. What started as a dream to create a space where people could connect, work, and play has grown into a thriving hub for students, professionals, and gamers alike.


Alice's commitment to exceptional service and creating a welcoming atmosphere has made Trivmacz the go-to destination for anyone needing reliable internet, a comfortable workspace, or a place to enjoy gaming with friends.
